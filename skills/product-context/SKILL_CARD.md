# Skill Card: product-context

## Description
Builds a compact, evidence-backed product context for later marketing and product work.

## Owner
ABVX / Anton Biletskiy-Volokh

## License
MIT. See repository LICENSE.

## Intended Use
Use before positioning, SEO, content, analytics, sales-enablement, or launch work when the product, audience, proof, and claim boundaries need a durable source.

## Out of Scope
Do not use as a substitute for product strategy, legal review, customer research, or publication approval.

## Sources and Attribution
Adapted from the shared `product-marketing` context pattern in `coreyhaines31/marketingskills`.

## Inputs and Outputs
Inputs: repo docs, public copy, implementation surfaces, analytics contracts, and owner-confirmed facts.

Outputs: a compact product context with proof boundaries, sources, and maintenance date.

## Risks and Mitigations
- Risk: invented commercial claims. Mitigation: separate confirmed facts from assumptions and require owner confirmation.
- Risk: durable context drift. Mitigation: record source surfaces and review after material product changes.
- Risk: private data leakage. Mitigation: exclude customer, sales, and personal data from committed files.

## Evaluation
Evaluated by structural validation and review against repos with public, private-pilot, and regulated-adjacent product surfaces.
